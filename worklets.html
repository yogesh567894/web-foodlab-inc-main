DepthProbe: for Accurate Depth and Radius Estimation of Circular Water Bodies

Abstract

This paper detects abandoned borewells in remote areas using OpenCV and computer vision. Borehole Depth Probes measure depth accurately. The system handles challenges like occlusions and lighting, making it useful in hydrogeological exploration, environmental monitoring, and disaster response planning. It advances automated hole detection and data-driven decision-making for resource management.

Performance analysis of Ground to UAV FSO communication with Energy Harvesting

Abstract

This research explores UAV-based free-space optical (FSO) communication, which offers high bandwidth and connectivity for applications like disaster management and structural inspection. FSO links between UAVs and network nodes can provide fast data rates without 5G interference. However, atmospheric factors can affect FSO performance. The study proposes analytical expressions to calculate harvested energy, aiming to improve FSO link design and energy efficiency, especially in ground-to-UAV FSO communication.
